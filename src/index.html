Dr. Kwame Nkrumah

First Prime Minister of Ghana

Here is a list of some of the achievements of Dr. Nkrumah:

  • Independence: - In 6th of March, 1957, Dr. Kwame Nkrumah and the Big Six and faught and gained independence for Ghana from the British rule and became the first Prime Minster of Ghana.
  • Development:- it was during his regime that Ghana recorded high number of social services such as schools, hospitals, and roads. Ghana became the first African country to provide free education and healthcare for her people.
  • Pan-Africanism: - Internationally, Nkrumah relentlessly pursued his idea of Pan-Africanism and emphasised the need for African Unity. This greatly contributed to the formation of the OAU in 1963.
